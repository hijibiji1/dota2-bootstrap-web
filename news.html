Spring Cleaning Update

There's nothing creeps enjoy more than swapping out swords for brooms and sweeping up the barracks. Fortunately for us, that means it's time to enjoy the fruits of their janitorial efforts as today's patch opens the windows and doors of Dota for the Spring Cleaning Update for 2022 — focused on bug fixes and a passel of quality-of-life improvements.

The Stockholm Major Fantasy

The Eastern Europe Qualifier tournament is now complete after 5 days of intense Dota. Congratulations to BetBoom, Team Spirit, and Mind Games who will now join teams from the around the world at the Stockholm Major.

As spring winds arrive to help break winter's final grip, it's time to welcome a new seasonal Dota Plus update — ushering with it new Plus features arriving from the latest Battle Pass, updated seasonal quests and guild rewards, and an all-new treasure to explore.

Latest Patch Update

Dota 2 Update MARCH 12, 2022

  • Game State Integration now requires the command-line option "-gamestateintegration" to function. This is because there can be a per-frame performance impact to using game state integration.

Gameplay Update FEBRUARY 25, 2022

  • Primal Beast turn rate increased from 0.5 to 0.6
  • Onslaught run speed increased from 1150 to 1200
  • Trample mana cost decreased from 90/95/100/105 to 90/85/80/75
  • Trample cooldown decreased from 30/28/26/24 to 30/27/24/21
  • Trample base damage increased from 20/30/40/50 to 20/35/50/65
  • Trample now has correct dispel rules
  • Uproar stack duration increased from 15s to 20s
  • Pulverize now works with items that increase cast range
  • Primal Beast Level 20 Trample damage talent increased from +20% to +30%

Dota 2 Update NOVEMBER 4, 2021

  • Fixed a bug with tower target selection when the tower's current target is very near the limit of a tower's attack range.
